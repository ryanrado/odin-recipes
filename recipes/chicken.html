High-Protein Chicken & Waffles

Craving Chicken & Waffles? We got you! This recipe uses Kodiak mix for protein-packed waffles and air-fried chicken for a healthier twist. 60g+ protein, less than 40g carbs, and under 10g added sugar! Perfect for a satisfying brunch.

    Waffles

  • 1 cup Kodiak Buttermilk Power Cakes Flapjack & Waffle Mix (or your favorite Kodiak protein mix)
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or your preferred milk)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

    • Chicken

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into tenders
  • 1/2 cup Kodiak Buttermilk Power Cakes Flapjack & Waffle Mix (or leftover mix from waffles)
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking Spray

    Instructions

  1. Make the Waffles: In a large bowl, whisk together Kodiak mix, almond milk, egg, applesauce, baking powder, and cinnamon. Let the batter rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.expand_more Spray with non-stick cooking spray if necessary.exclamation Pour batter evenly onto the waffle iron and cook until golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
  3. Prepare the Chicken: Preheat your air fryer to 400°F (200°C).expand_more In a shallow bowl, combine the leftover Kodiak mix, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Dredge each chicken tender in the dry mixture, then dip it in a bowl of beaten egg (optional for extra crispy coating) and coat again in the dry mixture.
  5. Arrange the breaded chicken tenders in a single layer in the air fryer basket, leaving space between them.expand_more Spray lightly with cooking spray.
  6. Air fry for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown, flipping halfway through. You can check the internal temperature with a meat thermometer – it should reach 165°F (74°C)
